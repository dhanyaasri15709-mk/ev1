About Mudumalai National Park

Mudumalai National Park is located in the Nilgiris district of Tamil Nadu, India, bordering Karnataka and Kerala. It is part of the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve and is famous for its rich biodiversity, including tigers, elephants, leopards, and numerous bird species.
