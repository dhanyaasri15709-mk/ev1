üíß Major Waterfalls

  • Main Falls (Peraruvi): The most popular and largest of all, located near the town center. The water here is said to contain herbs from the forests.
  • Five Falls (Aintharuvi): Water splits into five streams as it cascades down, resembling the five heads of a cobra.
  • Old Courtallam Falls (Pazhaya Courtallam): A peaceful spot with gentle falls ideal for family visits.
  • Tiger Falls (Puliyaruvi): A small but scenic fall, great for swimming and picnicking.
  • Shenbagadevi Falls: Located near a temple dedicated to Goddess Shenbagadevi; accessible by trekking.
  • Honey Falls (Thenaruvi): Known for its height and purity, located deep in the forest.
  • Orchard Falls (Pazhathotta Aruvi): Surrounded by fruit orchards, providing a refreshing and scenic atmosphere.
  • Sitraruvi (Small Falls): A smaller version of Main Falls, popular among locals.
  • Chitraruvi: Another beautiful cascade located nearby.

üå≥ Ecological Importance

The region around Courtallam is part of the Western Ghats, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its biodiversity. The forests surrounding the falls host:

  • Medicinal herbs and plants
  • Diverse bird species
  • Rare butterflies and insects
  • Freshwater fish and amphibians

The waterfalls are fed by the Chittar River, ensuring year-round water flow, especially during the monsoon season.
