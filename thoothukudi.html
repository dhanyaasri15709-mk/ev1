Thoothukudi Port, also known as Tuticorin Port, is a major seaport located on the southeastern coast of India in Tamil Nadu. It is one of the oldest ports in India, with a history dating back to the 18th century, and has developed into a modern deep-water port. The port handles cargo such as coal, salt, fertilizers, edible oils, and container traffic, making it a key hub for trade and commerce in southern India.
